# 🚀 Orion-X
### The Future of Personalized Learning

---

## 🛑 The Problem
### LMS. What's wrong?
1. **Static Content**: PDFs are just text. They don't talk back.
2. **One-Size-Fits-All**: Every student gets the same experience, regardless of their pace.
3. **Teacher Workload**: Creating quizzes and study guides is time-consuming.
4. **Engagement Gap**: Students often feel lost in complex technical materials.

---

## ✅ The Solution: Orion-X
### Generative AI at the Core

- **Dynamic Content Ingestion**: Turns PDFs into interactive knowledge stores.
- **AI-Powered Personalization**: Generates custom study roadmaps.
- **Real-Time Interactivity**: An AI Tutor that literally knows your course.
- **Automated Assessment**: Quizzes generated in seconds, not hours.

---

## 👨‍🏫 Key Features: For Teachers
### Elevate the Teaching Experience

- **Smart Ingestion**: Upload PDFs, let AI extract the essence.
- **AI Quiz Architect**: Generate MCQs using Google Gemini 2.0 Flash.
- **Virtual Classrooms**: Built-in Jitsi Meet integration for instant live sessions.
- **Performance Analytics**: Instant feedback and scoring for student progress.

---

## 🎓 Key Features: For Students
### Personalized, AI-Driven Growth

- **Interactive AI Tutor (RAG)**: Ask anything about your notes.
- **Study Roadmaps**: Get summaries, key terms, and study estimates.
- **Real-time Quizzing**: Challenge yourself with AI-generated tests.
- **Premium UI/UX**: Smooth transitions and modern dark-mode aesthetic.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
### Robust, Scalable, Modern

- **Frontend**: React 19, Framer Motion, Lucide Icons.
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python), SQLAlchemy.
- **AI Orchestration**: Google Gemini Pro, retrieval-augmented generation.
- **Database**: PostgreSQL / Supabase for secure data storage.
- **Cloud Storage**: Cloudinary for high-speed module hosting.

---

## 🧠 Why Orion-X?
### A Different Kind of Learning

Unlike traditional LMS, **Orion-X** doesn't just host content; it **understands** it. By bridging the gap between static documents and active learning, we empower students to learn faster and teachers to focus on what matters: **Mentorship.**
